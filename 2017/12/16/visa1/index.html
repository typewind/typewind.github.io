Tier 2 Immigration Statisic Analysis - Overview

Posted on 2017-12-16 |


As a Non-EEA citizen without work permit, I faced the most desperate situation during the job searching in my life (without one of): nearly 200 apply, and more than 90% of them never reply because I don’t have the work visa. So, what is the work visa? And How difficult is it for a foreigner to get a job in UK without the work visa?

Hope this (and following) post can help the people who is also struggling and dreaming to work in UK because of any kind of interest and fever.

What is work visa and how to get it?

Work visa, or Tier 2 (General) Visa is provide for the people who come from Non-EEA country and already get a offer from a employer with sponsor license.

The best way to describe the difficulty is let the data tell the true story. I’m illustrating the application situation of Tier 2 Work Visa of UK based on the Immigration Statistics which published by UKVI.
